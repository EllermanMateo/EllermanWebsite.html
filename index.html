On the other hand, supporters like Barbara van S. and Dominic Girard highlight the consequences of blocking and discrimination as well as network traffic which can be supported through a European experiment with an "open Internet." However, based on the podcast findings, most Americans favor an anti-net neutrality which a Forbes article focuses on three main key points. Likewise, more liberals call towards privacy against government regulation in the Internet as well as more freedom.
